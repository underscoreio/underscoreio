On-site and Online Courses

Privately booked courses can be run on-site or online:

Onsite courses are taught intensively at your offices over one or two days. Our trainers cover everything face-to-face with your team in one sitting.

Online courses are run a little a day over several days. Our trainers cover the material via video chat, and you complete coursework on Github. We give every participant one-on-one feedback via code review.
