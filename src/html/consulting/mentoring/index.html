Faster Progress for Your Team

What is Pairing?

Pair Programming involves two developers working together on a task, giving:

  • Knowledge transfer
  • Higher quality design
  • Less defects
  • Increased team engagement

What You Can Expect

Pairing needs to occur frequently to make a lasting difference. Team Mentoring is per-pair, and is for one hour every working day for a month.

  1. Each day the team pair. One half is an Underscore consultant, the other is one of your team members.
  2. This is remote, via video and audio Google Hangouts or Screen Hero.
  3. The session is driven by the needs and questions of your team member.
  4. The content may involve discussion, coding, exploration, joint problem solving, tooling, and documentation.
  5. Each week we report back to you relevant findings and suggestions.
