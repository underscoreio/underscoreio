Mentoring

Training is the perfect way to get your team up to speed quickly, but what do you do once the course is over? Our mentoring services are the answer.

Mentoring is the perfect compliment online and face-to-face training. Our Scala experts provide your developers with one-on-one support via face-to-face meetings, online hangouts, support emails, and code reviews. We help point you in the right direction, and you get on with the work at hand knowing you're using the best practices and technology choices for your project.
